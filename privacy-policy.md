# Privacy Policy — Cyrene Bot

**Last updated: June 7, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Cyrene Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and stores data when you use our services. By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

## 2. Data We Collect
The Bot collects the following data to provide its features:

- **Server IDs & Channel IDs** — Used to store server-specific configurations such as welcome messages, log channels, and temporary voice settings.
- **User IDs** — Used for features such as warnings, AFK status, reaction roles, voice channel ownership, and leaderboard tracking.
- **Usernames & Display Names** — Used temporarily during command execution (e.g. AFK status display).
- **Message Content** — Processed temporarily and in real-time for AFK detection and anti-scam filtering. Message content is **not** permanently stored.
- **Voice State Data** — Join and leave timestamps are tracked for the TempVoice leaderboard feature (total time spent in voice channels).

## 3. How We Use Your Data
Data collected by the Bot is used solely to provide and improve the Bot's features. We do not:
- Sell your data to third parties.
- Use your data for advertising purposes.
- Share your data with any external services.

## 4. Data Storage
All data is stored locally on the server hosting the Bot in JSON format. Data is retained as long as the Bot is active in your server or until you request deletion.

## 5. Data Deletion
Server administrators may request deletion of their server's data by contacting us through our official Discord support server. Upon removal of the Bot from your server, configuration data may be deleted upon request.

## 6. Third-Party Services
The Bot operates on Discord's platform and is subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy). We do not use any other third-party data processors.

## 7. Children's Privacy
The Bot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through our official Discord support server. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 9. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official Discord support server.
